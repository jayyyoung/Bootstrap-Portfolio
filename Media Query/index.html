About Me

Mucho gusto, me llamo Jason. I am 24 years old living out of Wickliffe, OH, a suburb of beautiful Cleveland. I have lived here my whole life and enjoy the massive changes in weather on a daily basis. I am a recent graduate of the University of Akron with a degree in graphic design, something I am currently employed in. I work for an online advertising/marketing company called Edge of the Box where manage and maintain auto dealership websites and produce their online advertising.

I am currently enrolled in the Case Western Reserve University's Coding Bootcamp where I look to excel in the world of, well, coding and web development. As of right now, I tend to lean towards the front-end of a website, making things look pretty. I am an artist at heart so I think that is where that stems from. All in all I hope to learn everything I can from Bootcamp and succeed in my endeavors from it.
